If you don't want to type the Redialed IP into WebTV Viewer every time you want to connect, follow these instructions:

1) Launch Viewer as you normally would
2) On the menu bar, click Edit, then click Preferences
3) Under "Web Mode Home Page", click the "Other" radio button.
4) Set the URL to the following:
file://disk/Browser/cSetup.htm
5) Click OK and relaunch Viewer

You should now see the updated Connect Setup page each time you launch Viewer. Just hit Connect and do `client:activ` as normal.
